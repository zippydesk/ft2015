Venue

Fabryka Trzciny Art Center, Otwocka 14, 02-759 Warsaw

Front-Trends 2015 will be held in The Fabryka Trzciny Art Center, located in an old, revamped factory in Warsaw. Built in 1916, it is one of the oldest post-industrial facets on the eastern side of Warsaw. Interiors still show the old architecture, but the old bones are now home to this modern and unorthodox cultural center.
